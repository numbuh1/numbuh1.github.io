maimai Plate Tracker

All Plates


For PC

1. Drag the link into the bookmarks bar to save the bookmarklet.

Plate Tracker Bookmarklet

2. Log in to maimai DX NET and click the bookmarklet in bookmarks bar.


For Mobile

1. Click on the button to copy the script in clipboard.

2. Add the current page to bookmarks.

3. Check "Bookmark" settings, replace the URL with what you copied earlier.

4. Rename the Bookmark ("Plate Tracker Bookmarklet")

5. Log in to maimai DX NET, type and select the Bookmark name on the URL Address bar
